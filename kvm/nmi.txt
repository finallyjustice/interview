This is about how to panic on unknown NMI.

1. Enable panic on unknown nmi:

# echo 1 > /proc/sys/kernel/unknown_nmi_panic

2. Trigger nmi from QEMU monitor:

(qemu) help nmi
nmi  -- inject an NMI
(qemu) nmi
